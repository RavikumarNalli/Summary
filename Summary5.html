IPS Manoj Sharma 
IPS officer Manoj Sharma and IRS officer Shraddha Joshi's inspiring love story serves as the inspiration for Vikrant Massey's film '12th Fail,' directed by Vidhu Vinod Chopra. The couple met at a UPSC coaching center in Delhi's Mukherjee Nagar and Manoj pursued Shraddha wholeheartedly.
